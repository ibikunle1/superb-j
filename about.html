    our history
    courtesy: school father/bursar

    The school was established in the year 2010. Facing various challenges, the school started because of the passion the founder as for teaching, in the living room of Mr. Ibikunle where it all began, she would take extra lesson for kids in her viccinity when she comes back from work. Then on a fateful day she decided to found her own school starting with few kids who comes to her lesson and she began to establish until she built a standard schooling environment and moved in the year 2015.

    Superb J as it is fondly referred to as attained autonomous and independent status in 2017 by virtue of the Ogun State Government Approval which granted it the exclusive mandate to provide Social, Practical and theory learning and training in School.

    The School is well acknowledged both within and outside its environment as a pace setter and a unique Centre of Learning and Teaching. It has forged ahead and repositioned itself as an institute of class and excellence.
